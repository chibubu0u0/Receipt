Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Song Receipt Studio collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the service.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information: email address, authentication status, and account identifiers.
  • Generation inputs: artist name, song title, optional notes, selected style, selected output size, and related prompts.
  • Generated content: generated receipt data, visual output settings, and saved generation history.
  • Credit and purchase records: credit balance, credit usage logs, order IDs, payment provider references, package purchased, and payment status.
  • Technical information: browser type, device information, log data, and basic analytics or error information needed to operate the service.

2. How we use information

We use information to provide login, generate receipts, deduct and add credits, save generation history, process purchases, prevent abuse, investigate errors, improve product reliability, and respond to support requests.

3. Third-party processors

Provider Purpose
Supabase Authentication, database, user credits, generation history, and purchase records.
OpenAI AI generation of receipt content based on user-provided inputs.
Vercel Website hosting, serverless API functions, and deployment infrastructure.
Paddle or other payment providers Checkout, payment processing, fraud prevention, tax handling, invoices, and payment notifications.

4. Payment data

We do not store full credit card numbers or sensitive payment credentials. Payment providers handle checkout and may share payment status, order IDs, transaction references, and limited billing information necessary to complete purchases and provide support.

5. Cookies and local storage

The service may use cookies, local storage, or similar browser technologies to keep you logged in, remember interface state, and support essential app functionality.

6. Data retention

We retain account, credit, purchase, and generation history as long as needed to provide the service, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, maintain accurate accounting, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Your choices

You may request access, correction, export, or deletion of your account-related information by contacting us. Some records may need to be retained for security, payment, tax, dispute, or legal reasons.

8. International processing

Our providers may process information in countries outside your location. By using the service, you understand that your information may be processed internationally.
