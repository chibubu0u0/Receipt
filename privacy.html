Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Song Receipt Studio collects, uses, and protects information when you use our digital service.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information, such as your email address.
  • Generation inputs, such as artist name, song title, and optional notes.
  • Generated receipt data and generation history.
  • Payment-related records, such as order status, credit package, and payment provider transaction references.

2. How we use information

We use information to provide account login, generate receipts, manage credits, store generation history, process purchases, prevent abuse, and improve the service experience.

3. Third-party services

We use third-party providers to operate the service, including Supabase for authentication and database features, OpenAI for AI generation, Vercel for hosting, and payment providers such as Paddle or other supported processors for checkout and payment confirmation.

4. Payment information

We do not store full credit card numbers or sensitive payment credentials. Payments are handled by third-party payment processors according to their own security and privacy practices.

5. Data retention

We retain account, credit, payment, and generation history information as needed to operate the service, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and comply with applicable obligations.

6. Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your account-related information, subject to legal, security, and operational requirements.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, please contact: privacy@songreceipt.studio
