Refund Policy

Song Receipt Studio sells digital generation credits. Credits are delivered to your account after successful payment confirmation and may be consumed immediately to generate AI receipt outputs.

1. Unused credits

Refund requests for unused credits may be reviewed within 14 days of purchase. Approval depends on account status, payment status, usage history, and the reason for the request.

2. Used credits

Used credits are generally non-refundable because they represent consumed digital service capacity and AI generation costs.

3. Failed or duplicate payments

If you believe you were charged by mistake, charged twice, or did not receive purchased credits after a confirmed payment, please contact us with your account email and payment reference.

4. Abuse and policy violations

Refunds may be declined if there is evidence of abuse, fraud, account misuse, chargeback abuse, or violation of the Terms of Service.

5. How to request a refund

Please contact: support@songreceipt.studio with your account email, purchase date, credit package, and reason for the request.
