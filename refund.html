Refund Policy

Song Receipt Studio sells digital generation credits. Credits are delivered electronically and may be consumed immediately to generate AI receipt outputs.

1. General policy

Refunds are reviewed case by case. Because credits are digital consumable items, used credits are generally non-refundable. This does not affect any mandatory rights you may have under applicable consumer protection laws.

2. Unused credits

If you purchased credits by mistake and have not used them, you may request a refund within 14 days of purchase. We may review the request based on payment status, credit usage, account activity, and the reason for the request.

3. Used credits

Credits that have already been used for successful AI generations are generally non-refundable because they represent consumed digital service capacity, compute costs, and delivered digital output.

4. Failed delivery or duplicate payment

If your payment succeeded but credits were not added, or if you believe you were charged twice, contact us with your account email, purchase date, payment reference, and package purchased. We will investigate and either deliver the missing credits or issue an appropriate refund where applicable.

5. Abuse, fraud, and violations

Refunds may be declined where there is evidence of fraud, refund abuse, chargeback abuse, policy violations, or misuse of the credit system.

6. Payment provider handling

Some refunds may be processed through Paddle or another payment provider used at checkout. Processing times depend on the payment method, payment provider, and banking network.

7. How to request a refund

Email chibubux3@gmail.com with:

  • Your account email
  • Purchase date
  • Credit package purchased
  • Payment or order reference
  • Reason for the request

Tip: If your payment succeeded but credits do not appear immediately, refresh your account after a few moments. Payment webhooks can occasionally take a short time to complete.
